What do we do?

We basically work like any other reverse image search websites, except we also give exclusive watermarks to you so as to ensure nobody would plagiarize your artwork!

Quite literally, DupliArt is a reverse image search engine that works just like any other reverse image website such as Google Image and Plaghunter. Though, we focus more on artworks by artists and whoever posts their artworks online.

How to Use?

  • 1. Sign up or login to keep up with us!
  • 2. Upload your artwork by clicking on the "Upload picture" button.
  • 3. Similar images might or might not appear; but when you found the suspicious one, click on that image.
  • 4. Depending whether that person really plagiarize your work or not, proceed on to click at the "Report" button.
  • 5. Leave the rest to us and we'll get back to you!

About us

DupliArt is a temporary reverse image search website engine created by four Multimedia students from University of Malaya for a project assignment.
